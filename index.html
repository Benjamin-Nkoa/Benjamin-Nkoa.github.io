SmartFib identifies significant swing highs and lows using a customizable ZigZag algorithm. These swings define the trend (HH-HL / LH-LL) and the Fibonacci measurement range.

Fibonacci Level Calculation

From the most recent swings, SmartFib automatically calculates retracement levels, targeting specifically the 0.705–1 range — the last barrier for a rebound during a retracement.

Within the Fibonacci zone, the EA scans for Order Blocks (the last bearish candle before a bullish impulse, or vice versa) representing institutional interest areas.
